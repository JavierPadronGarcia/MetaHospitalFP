CREATION:


To create a new user you have to fill in completely the form on the right side of the screen, in it you will write the name, email, 

you can choose between the available roles and select a school (necessary to create students and teachers).


Once the registration is created, a notification will appear with different content depending on whether the user has failed or has been successfully created,



Furthermore, at the same time as it is successfully created, the new user will receive an email with their username and password, with which they will be able to log in and access the platform.


UPDATE:




To update you will need to click on the pencil icon in the Actions column.

Once clicked, all the user data will be transported to the form on the right side of the screen, where the necessary fields will be edited and to confirm the update, click on Submit.

In case you want to cancel the update, click on the "Cancel" button, this will remove all the data from the form and you will be able to add users again.


In the same way as the creation of users, a notification with the status will appear when the update is done.


REMOVE:


To delete users (THIS ACTION CANNOT BE REMOVED) you have to click on the bin icon in the "Actions" column, this will be the last icon in the row.

Once clicked, a semall confirmation window will appear, clicking on "Yes" will permanently delete the selected user, and, similar to the other actions, a notification will appear.
