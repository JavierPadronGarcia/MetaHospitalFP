This guide has functionality for actions on students and faculty, so both sections have the same use.



CREATING:


To create a new student you have to fill in completely the form on the right side of the screen, in it you will write the name and the email address


Once the registration is created, a notification will appear with different content depending on whether the student has failed or has been successfully created,



At the same time as it is successfully created, the new user will receive an email with their username and password, with which they will be able to log in and access the platform.



UPDATE:


To update you will have to click on the pencil icon in the Actions column.



Once clicked, all the user data will be transported to the form on the right hand side of the screen, where the necessary fields will be edited and to confirm the update, click on Submit.


In case you want to cancel the update, click on the "Cancel" button, this will remove all the data from the form and you will be able to add users again.




REMOVE:


To delete students (THIS ACTION CANNOT BE REMOVED) click on the bin icon in the "Actions" column, this will be the last icon in the row.
