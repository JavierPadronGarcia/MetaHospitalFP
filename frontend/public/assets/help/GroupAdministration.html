Internal management of a specific group



CREATING:


To create groups it is necessary to write the name, the year in which it is going to be taken and the course of the group in the form on the right side of the screen, once written if you select "Send" a new group will be added, as well as receiving a message that it has been created correctly, if any error occurs it will be notified in the same way.



In addition, the content of the specific group can be accessed by clicking on the name of the group and you will navigate to the corresponding group, so that you are already in the group view.



UPDATE:


A la hora de actualizar, hay que hace clic en el icono del lápiz en la columna de "Acciones" y se transportarán todos los datos al formulario para poder cambiarlo, al hacer clic en "Enviar" se procederá a la actualización del registro, además de recibir una notificación de igual manera que las otras acciones




REMOVE:


To delete groups (THIS ACTION CANNOT BE REMOVED) click on the bin icon in the "Actions" column, this will be the last icon in the row.
